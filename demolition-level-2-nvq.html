NVQ Overview – Demolition Level 2

This Level 2 NVQ is designed for demolition operatives working on demolition sites. You’ll demonstrate your skills, knowledge, and experience using our online portfolio. It’s the recognised route to the Blue CSCS Skilled Worker Card (combined with an in‑date CITB HS&E test).

What the NVQ covers:
  • Preparing and controlling demolition operations
  • Using hand tools and mechanical equipment
  • Sorting and removing materials
  • Following health, safety and environmental procedures

What to expect from the NVQ

You’ll fill in a registration form and provide a copy of your photo ID. Once enrolled, your assessor schedules an induction to explain the portfolio and answer questions. You can start any day – no fixed intake.

You’ll use our online portfolio system. Your assessor will ask you to complete knowledge questions (written or via discussion) and gather workplace evidence: photographs, videos of you working, copies of site documents, and a witness testimony from a competent person.

When your assessor is satisfied that all evidence meets the standards, they’ll submit for internal verification. You’ll receive your CITB‑approved NVQ diploma – usually within 4 weeks if you work consistently.

This NVQ is for people already employed as demolition operatives or in similar roles. All evidence must come from real site work – it cannot be simulated. You need to be working on site to enrol.

You need to complete a set of mandatory units covering core demolition skills, plus optional units depending on your role. Your assessor will help you choose the right combination based on your daily work.
