About the CSCS Gold Supervisor Card

The CSCS Gold Supervisor Card is designed for experienced construction supervisors and foremen who are responsible for overseeing workers, managing tasks and maintaining safety standards on construction sites across the United Kingdom.

This Gold CSCS card demonstrates to employers and principal contractors throughout England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland that you are qualified, experienced and meet the required health and safety standards to supervise work safely and effectively.

CSCS Gold Supervisor Card Requirements

  • Successfully pass the CITB Health, Safety & Environment (HS&E) Test for Supervisors at a UK test centre within the preceding 24 months.
  • Hold a relevant NVQ or SVQ Level 3 (or higher) in a suitable construction-related supervisory or technical qualification recognised within the UK.
  • Provide valid UK proof of identity, such as a passport or driving licence.
  • Be able to demonstrate supervisory experience and responsibilities on UK construction sites.

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Complete our online application form or contact our UK support team via email to apply.
  2. Ensure your CITB HS&E Supervisor Test has been passed at a UK test centre within the last 2 years.
  3. Provide evidence of your NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or higher supervisory qualification recognised in the UK.
  4. Submit your proof of identity and any additional documents requested.
  5. Receive confirmation of your application and dispatch updates.
  6. Obtain your CSCS Gold Supervisor Card delivered to your UK address and utilise it to access sites as a recognised supervisor.

Frequently Asked Questions – CSCS Gold Supervisor Card

Who is the CSCS Gold Supervisor Card intended for?

The Gold Supervisor card is designed for experienced construction supervisors, foremen and individuals in comparable roles who are responsible for managing workers and maintaining safety on UK construction sites.

What is the validity period of the CSCS Gold Supervisor Card?

The CSCS Gold Supervisor Card is typically valid for 5 years within the United Kingdom. Renewal is required prior to expiry, and your CITB test must remain current.

Is an NVQ/SVQ mandatory for obtaining a Gold Supervisor Card?

Yes. You generally require an appropriate NVQ or SVQ at Level 3 or above in a suitable construction supervisory or technical qualification recognised in the UK to qualify for the Gold Supervisor card.

Which CITB test is necessary for UK supervisors?

You will typically need to pass the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Supervisors at a UK test centre within the last 2 years before applying for the CSCS Gold Supervisor Card.

What is the expected delivery timeframe for my CSCS Gold Supervisor Card?

Following application approval and payment completion, your card is usually dispatched within approximately 5–6 working days to your UK address.
