NVQ Overview – Access Flooring Level 2

This Level 2 NVQ is designed for tradespeople installing access flooring systems in commercial and industrial settings. You’ll demonstrate your skills, knowledge, and experience using our online portfolio. It’s the recognised route to the Blue CSCS Skilled Worker Card (combined with an in‑date CITB HS&E test).

What the NVQ covers:
  • Installing access flooring panels and pedestals
  • Cutting and fitting floor panels
  • Working to drawings and specifications
  • Following health, safety and welfare procedures

What to expect from the NVQ

You’ll fill in a registration form and provide ID. Once enrolled, your assessor schedules an induction to explain the portfolio.

You’ll use our online portfolio system. Your assessor guides you through evidence gathering: photos, videos, witness testimonies.

Your assessor submits for verification. You receive your CITB‑approved NVQ diploma – usually within 4 weeks.

This NVQ is for people already employed as access flooring installers. Evidence must come from real site work.
