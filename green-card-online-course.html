About the Level 1 Health, Safety & Awareness Course

You’ll learn the essentials to stay safe and compliant on any UK construction site. Modules cover:

  • Health and safety law in construction (British standards)
  • Risk assessments and method statements (how to spot hazards)
  • Accident prevention and RIDDOR reporting
  • Your role in site safety – from worker to supervisor
  • Fire prevention, manual handling, and working at height basics
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements

No jargon overload – just practical knowledge you’ll use every day. The course is designed for speakers of English as a first or second language, with clear voiceovers and simple assessments.
