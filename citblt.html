Test Information & Requirements

About the CITB Operatives Test

The CITB Health, Safety & Environment Operatives Test is required for anyone applying for a CSCS Green Labourer Card. The test assesses your knowledge of health and safety practices on UK construction sites.

Important Information

Your CITB test pass is valid for 2 years from the test date. You must apply for your CSCS card within this period to avoid retaking the test.

Test Format & Details

  • Duration: 45-minute computer-based test
  • Questions: 50 multiple-choice questions
  • Pass Mark: 45 out of 50 (90% minimum)
  • Results: Immediate notification upon completion
  • Topics: Health, safety and environment in construction
  • Languages: Available in English and multiple languages

What to Bring to Your Test

  • Valid photo ID (passport or UK driving licence)
  • Your booking confirmation (digital or printed)
  • National Insurance number (if available)
  • Reading glasses if needed

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my CSCS Labourer Card?

Once you've passed your CITB Operatives Test, you can apply for your CSCS Labourer Card through constructionscert. The card typically arrives within 5-7 working days after your application is approved.

What if I fail the CITB Operatives Test?

If you don't pass the test on your first attempt, you can retake it after a 7-day waiting period. We recommend using this time to review the CSCS study materials available from CITB.

How long is the CITB Operatives Test valid for?

Your CITB Operatives Test pass is valid for 2 years. You must apply for your CSCS Labourer Card within this timeframe to avoid retaking the test.

Can I change my test date after booking?
