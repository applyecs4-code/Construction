Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a CSCS Card online?

You can apply for a CSCS card online by choosing your card type, booking the required CITB HS&E test and submitting your application details through Constructions Cert.

Can you book the CITB test for me?

Yes, Constructions Cert helps you book the correct CITB Health, Safety and Environment test for operatives, specialists or managers and professionals.

Do you help with CSCS card renewals?

We provide full support for renewing most CSCS cards, including checking your existing card, booking the right CITB test and guiding you through the renewal process.

What is the process for CSCS Test Booking?

The process involves selecting your test type, choosing a UK test centre location, and scheduling your test date.

How long is a CSCS Card valid for?

Most UK CSCS Cards are valid for 5 years. Some temporary cards like the Provisional card are valid for 6 months and cannot be renewed.
