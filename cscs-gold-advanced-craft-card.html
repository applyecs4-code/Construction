About the CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card

The CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card is designed for highly skilled tradespeople who have achieved an advanced level of competence in their trade, usually proven by an NVQ or SVQ Level 3 or a City & Guilds Advanced Craft qualification.

This Gold Advanced Craft CSCS card shows UK employers and main contractors that you are qualified, experienced and able to carry out complex work safely and to a high standard on construction sites across the UK.

Who Can Apply for the CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card?

The Gold Advanced Craft card is aimed at experienced tradespeople who have advanced qualifications and experience in areas such as:

  • Painting and decorating
  • Plastering and dry lining
  • Carpentry and joinery
  • Bricklaying and masonry
  • Cladding and exterior finishes
  • Other specialist trades with advanced skills and responsibility

If you are a highly skilled worker with a recognised advanced qualification, this card can help you prove your level and progress your UK construction career.

CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card Requirements

  • Pass the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test (Operatives or Specialist as appropriate) within the last 2 years.
  • Hold an NVQ/SVQ Level 3 in a relevant trade or a City & Guilds Advanced Craft qualification.
  • Provide proof of identity, such as a UK passport or driving licence.
  • Be able to demonstrate sufficient experience at an advanced craft level in your trade.

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Complete our online application form at constructionscert or contact our support team
  2. Confirm that your CITB HS&E Test has been passed within the last 2 years.
  3. Submit evidence of your NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or City & Guilds Advanced Craft qualification.
  4. Provide identity documents and any additional supporting information requested.
  5. Receive confirmation of your application and dispatch updates by email.
  6. Get your CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card delivered to your UK address, usually within around 5–6 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions – Gold Advanced Craft CSCS Card

Who is eligible for the CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card?

Generally, you need an NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or City & Guilds Advanced Craft Certificate in a relevant trade, plus a valid CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test result.

How long is the CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card valid?

The card is normally valid for 5 years. You will need to renew it and keep your CITB test result in date to maintain site access.

Can I renew or replace a lost Gold Advanced Craft card?

Yes. You can apply for a renewal or replacement online through constructionscert, and cards are usually posted within around 5–6 working days once approved.

Is the Gold Advanced Craft card accepted on all sites?

The CSCS Gold Advanced Craft Card is widely accepted across UK construction sites as proof of advanced skill and competence, but individual site rules may still apply.
