Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get my CSCS card through constructionscert?

Processing times vary by card type and individual qualifications. Most standard CSCS card applications are processed within 5-10 working days after successful completion of the required CITB test. Express processing options may be available for urgent requirements.

What documents are needed for CSCS card applications?

Standard requirements include valid photo identification, proof of address, and relevant qualification certificates. Specific requirements vary depending on the CSCS card type being applied for. Our team can provide detailed guidance based on your specific circumstances.

Does constructionscert provide CITB test preparation support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive preparation resources including study guides, practice tests, and revision materials for the CITB Health, Safety and Environment test. Contact us to learn about our preparation packages designed to help candidates succeed.

Can constructionscert arrange testing for company teams?

Absolutely. We provide group booking services for organizations and companies requiring multiple certifications. Contact our corporate services team to discuss customized solutions for your workforce certification needs.
