About the CSCS Green Labourer Card

The CSCS Green Labourer Card is the primary entry-level CSCS card for individuals undertaking general labouring duties on UK construction sites. It verifies that you possess the essential health and safety knowledge required to work safely under supervision.

This Green CSCS card is widely recognised by employers throughout the United Kingdom and helps you comply with site regulations, enabling you to begin or continue your construction career with confidence.

CSCS Green Labourer Card Requirements

Requirements for New Applicants

  • Successfully pass the CITB Health, Safety & Environment (HS&E) Operatives Test within the preceding 24 months.
  • Complete an accredited Level 1 Health & Safety in a Construction Environment qualification (or accepted equivalent).
  • Provide valid UK identification, such as a passport or driving licence.
  • Demonstrate capability to work under supervision on construction sites.

Requirements for Renewal Applications

  • Retake and pass the CITB HS&E Operatives Test if your current test certificate has expired.
  • Submit evidence of updated Health & Safety training if required.
  • Complete your renewal application through our online portal or via email.

Frequently Asked Questions – CSCS Green Labourer Card

Who requires a CSCS Green Labourer Card?

Individuals performing basic labouring or site support duties on UK construction sites are typically expected to hold a CSCS Green Labourer Card to demonstrate compliance with minimum safety standards.

What is the validity period of the CSCS Green Labourer Card?

The CSCS Green Labourer Card is generally valid for 5 years from the issue date. Renewal is required upon expiry, alongside maintaining a valid CITB test certificate.

Can I transfer my CSCS Green Labourer Card to another person?

No. Each CSCS card is personally assigned and non-transferable. Sharing or transferring cards contravenes CSCS regulations.

What work activities does the CSCS Green Labourer Card permit?

It authorises you to work as a labourer on UK construction sites, performing basic tasks such as site clearance, material handling and trade assistance, while operating under appropriate supervision.

What are the costs associated with obtaining a CSCS Green Labourer Card?

Costs vary depending on training providers and application methods. Email support@constructionscert.co.uk for current information regarding CITB test fees and card application charges.
