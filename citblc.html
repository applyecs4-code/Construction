Everything You Need to Know

About the CITB Operatives Test

The CITB Health, Safety & Environment Operatives Test is required for anyone applying for a CSCS Green Labourer Card. The test assesses your knowledge of health and safety practices on construction sites across the UK.

Important Note

Your CITB test pass is valid for 2 years from the test date. You must apply for your CSCS card within this period to avoid retaking the test.

Test Format & Details

  • Duration: 45-minute computer-based test
  • Questions: 50 multiple-choice questions
  • Pass Mark: 45 out of 50 (90% minimum)
  • Results: Immediate notification upon completion
  • Topics: Health, safety and environment in construction
  • Languages: Available in English and multiple languages

What to Bring to Your Test

  • Valid photo ID (passport or driving licence)
  • Your booking confirmation (digital or printed)
  • National Insurance number (if available)
  • Reading glasses if needed
