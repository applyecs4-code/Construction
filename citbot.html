About the CITB Operatives Test

The CITB Health, Safety & Environment Operatives Test is required for anyone applying for most CSCS Cards. The test assesses your knowledge of health and safety practices on UK construction sites.

Important Information

Your CITB Operatives Test pass is valid for 2 years from the test date. You must apply for your CSCS card within this timeframe to avoid retaking the test.

Test Format & Details

  • Duration: 45-minute computer-based test
  • Questions: 50 multiple-choice questions
  • Pass Mark: 45 out of 50 (90% minimum)
  • Results: Immediate notification upon completion
  • Topics: Health, safety and environment in construction
  • Languages: Available in English and multiple languages

What to Bring to Your Test

  • Valid photo ID (UK passport or driving licence)
  • Your booking confirmation from constructionscert
  • National Insurance number (if available)
  • Reading glasses if required
