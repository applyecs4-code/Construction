What this wall and floor tiling NVQ covers

This Level 2 NVQ is designed for experienced tilers who fix wall and floor tiles in kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces. You’ll prove your competence through photos, short videos, and a chat with your assessor – no essays, no exams. It’s the most direct route to your Blue CSCS card and shows you’re skilled in:

Real tasks you’ll evidence:
  • Preparing surfaces (walls and floors) for tiling
  • Fixing tiles using adhesives, spacers, and grout
  • Cutting tiles to fit around pipes, sockets, and corners
  • Working to drawings and specifications
  • Following health, safety and environmental procedures

What to expect from the NVQ

You complete a simple registration form and provide a copy of your photo ID (passport or driving licence). Your assessor then arranges a short induction call to talk through the units, the portfolio system, and the kind of evidence that works best for your role. No exams, no college enrolment.

You’ll use our online portfolio to upload evidence: photos of you tiling walls and floors, site plans, risk assessments, and a witness testimony from your manager or client. You’ll also answer a few knowledge questions – you can write them or just discuss them with your assessor over the phone. They’re there to help, not to trip you up.

When your assessor is happy that all evidence meets the standards, they’ll arrange a final professional discussion – a relaxed chat about how you work, handle problems, and keep safe. After that, your portfolio is sent for internal verification, and you’ll receive your CITB‑approved diploma in the post. Then you’re ready to apply for your Blue CSCS card.
