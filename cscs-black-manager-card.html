About the CSCS Black Manager Card

The CSCS Black Manager Card is designed for experienced construction managers and senior professionals in the UK who are responsible for planning, managing and monitoring work on site.

This Black managers CSCS card confirms that you hold a recognised higher-level construction management qualification (usually NVQ Level 6 or 7) and have passed the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals.

CSCS Black Manager Card Eligibility Criteria

  • Pass the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals within the last 2 years.
  • Hold a relevant NVQ or SVQ Level 6 or 7 in Construction Management or an equivalent higher-level qualification.
  • Be working, or aspiring to work, in a management, leadership or senior professional role on UK construction projects.
  • Provide proof of identity (e.g. UK passport or driving licence).
  • Provide evidence of qualifications and UK construction management experience.

Renewing the CSCS Black Manager Card

The CSCS Black Manager Card is valid for 5 years. To renew your card and maintain your site access and professional status, you will need to:

  • Retake the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals (if your result has expired).
  • Provide any updated qualifications or management training certificates.
  • Complete a renewal application online through our portal.

Lost or Stolen Black Manager Card

If your CSCS Black Manager Card has been lost, stolen or damaged, you can request a replacement through our online system. Once processed, your replacement card is usually posted within 5–6 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions – CSCS Black Managers Card

Who can apply for the CSCS Black Manager Card?

Experienced construction managers, senior supervisors and professionals with a recognised construction management qualification (typically NVQ Level 6 or 7) and a valid CITB Managers & Professionals HS&E test result can apply.

Is the Black Manager Card the same as a managers and professionals CSCS card?

The CSCS Black Manager Card is the recognised CSCS card for managers and senior professionals who meet the qualification and CITB test requirements for management-level roles.

How long does it take to receive my card?

Once your application is approved, your CSCS Black Manager Card is normally dispatched within 5–6 working days to your UK address.

Can non-UK nationals apply for a CSCS Black Manager Card?

Yes, provided they hold recognised construction management qualifications, have the right to work in the UK and pass the CITB Managers & Professionals HS&E test.
