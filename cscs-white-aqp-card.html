About the CSCS White AQP Card

The CSCS White Academically Qualified Person (AQP) Card is specifically designed for construction professionals who hold recognised academic qualifications rather than vocational NVQs. This card formally recognises your academic achievements and demonstrates your commitment to professional standards in the UK construction industry.

Ideal for graduates, engineers, project managers, surveyors, and other academically qualified professionals, the AQP Card provides evidence of both your technical knowledge and understanding of UK construction health and safety requirements. It's widely accepted across UK construction sites for professional and management roles.

As the construction industry increasingly values formal education alongside practical experience, the AQP Card serves as a vital credential for career progression and professional recognition.

Eligibility Requirements

Academic Qualifications

Recognised construction-related degree, HND, HNC, CIOB qualification, NEBOSH diploma, or equivalent academic award in built environment disciplines.

Valid pass certificate for the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals (completed within last 2 years).

CSCS academic certificates or transcripts from recognised educational institutions, plus valid UK identification.

Accepted Academic Qualifications

Recognised Qualifications for AQP Card Application

  • Construction-related BSc, BEng, or MSc degrees (Civil Engineering, Construction Management, Architecture, etc.)
  • Higher National Diploma (HND) in construction or built environment subjects
  • Higher National Certificate (HNC) in relevant construction disciplines
  • Chartered Institute of Building (CIOB) qualifications
  • National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health (NEBOSH) diplomas
  • Other recognised professional qualifications in construction, engineering, or surveying
  • Equivalent international qualifications (subject to UK recognition)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between AQP Card and other CSCS cards?

The AQP Card recognises academic qualifications, while other CSCS cards (Green, Blue, Gold, Black) typically require vocational NVQs. The AQP Card is specifically for professionally qualified staff with academic backgrounds in construction-related disciplines.

Can I use my international degree for AQP Card application?

International degrees are considered if they are recognised as equivalent to UK qualifications in construction-related fields. Our professional team can advise on the recognition status of international qualifications.

How long is the AQP Card valid and how do I renew it?

The AQP Card is typically valid for 5 years. Renewal requires passing the latest CITB Managers & Professionals test and providing current evidence of your professional status. Academic qualifications generally don't need re-verification unless specifically requested.

What CITB test is required for AQP Card?

The CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals is required. This test focuses on the knowledge needed for supervisory and management roles in UK construction.

Can I upgrade from AQP Card to a different CSCS card?

Yes, you can progress to other CSCS cards as you gain additional qualifications and experience. Many professionals transition from AQP Cards to Black Manager Cards or other specialised cards as their careers progress.
