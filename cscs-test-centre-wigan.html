Opening hours

Opening Times – Wigan CSCS Test Centre

The Wigan HS&E test centre runs pre-booked sessions on weekdays. Exact times can vary, but typically:

  • Monday – Friday: test sessions usually between 8:00am and 4:00pm
  • Saturday: may open on selected dates if demand is high
  • Sunday & Bank Holidays: normally closed

Aim to arrive at least 15 minutes before your booked start time. This allows staff to check your ID, store personal belongings and explain the exam rules. If you arrive late, you may not be allowed to sit the test and the fee can be lost under CITB rules.

Test types

Which CSCS Tests Run at Wigan?

The Wigan Pearson VUE centre delivers the main CITB HS&E tests linked to CSCS cards:

  • Operatives HS&E Test – required for most Green Labourer and entry-level cards
  • Specialist HS&E Tests – used for many Blue and Gold skilled worker cards
  • Managers & Professionals (MAP) Test – for Black Manager cards, PQP and senior roles
  • Retake / renewal HS&E tests – when you need a new pass to renew or upgrade your card

If you're unsure which HS&E test matches your trade, NVQ level or card colour, the ConstructionsCert team can review your details and recommend the correct Wigan exam before you book.

Travel & transport

How to Get to the Wigan CSCS Test Centre

Wigan is well connected by rail, bus and major roads. Planning your route before test day helps you arrive calm and on time.

By Train

Wigan has two central stations: Wigan North Western and Wigan Wallgate. Both are a short walk from Market Street and the CSCS test centre.

  • From either station, follow signs towards Wigan town centre / Market Place / The Galleries.
  • Walk through the main shopping streets until you reach Market Street.
  • Look for number 38–40 Market Street and the Pearson VUE test centre entrance.

Walking time from the stations to Market Street is usually around 5–10 minutes.

By Bus

The test centre is very close to Wigan Bus Station. After you get off your bus, follow signs towards the town centre and cross into Market Street. The Pearson VUE centre at 38–40 Market Street is only a short walk from the bus station entrance.

By Car & Parking

If you are driving, use postcode WN1 1HX in your sat-nav and follow signs for Wigan town centre, the Galleries / Grand Arcade and Market Street.

There is no dedicated candidate parking at 38–40 Market Street. Use nearby town-centre car parks or pay-and-display bays, then walk to the test centre. Allow extra time to park, pay and reach the building before your check-in time.

Booking & ID rules

Booking Your CSCS Test in Wigan with ConstructionsCert

CITB has strict photographic ID rules for every HS&E test. At Wigan you must bring one original, valid form of photo ID, such as:

  • A current UK or international passport, or
  • A valid UK photocard driving licence

If you arrive at Market Street without acceptable ID, staff cannot let you sit the exam and your fee may be lost. Always re-check the ID requirements in your confirmation email a few days before your test date.

Results & next steps

After Your CSCS Test at Wigan

The HS&E test at Wigan is a computer-based exam with 50 multiple-choice questions and a time limit of around 45 minutes. Questions cover site hazards, safe working methods, health risks and your responsibilities under UK health and safety law.

Once you finish, the system marks your answers straight away and reception staff print a score report. This confirms whether you have passed and shows how you performed across the different topic areas.

If you pass, you can usually apply for your CSCS card immediately using the information on your report. If you do not pass this time, ConstructionsCert can help you book another HS&E test at Wigan or a nearby centre and point you towards realistic revision options to improve your confidence.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions – CSCS Test Centre in Wigan

1. What time should I arrive at the Wigan CSCS test centre?

Plan to arrive at Ground & Basement, 38–40 Market Street at least 15–20 minutes before your booked start time. This gives you time for ID checks, lockers and a short briefing so you are not rushing into the exam room.

2. Can I change or cancel my Wigan HS&E test?

CITB normally requires at least 72 hours' notice to move or cancel a HS&E booking without losing the fee. If you booked through ConstructionsCert and your plans change, contact us as soon as possible so we can explain the options for your Wigan appointment.

3. What should I bring on the day?

You must bring your valid photo ID (passport or UK photocard driving licence). It also helps to have your confirmation email or booking reference. Phones, smart watches, notes and other belongings are not allowed in the exam room and must be stored as directed by staff.

4. Is the Wigan test centre accessible?

The Wigan Pearson VUE centre occupies ground and basement floors. If you require step-free access, extra time, a separate room or other reasonable adjustments, tell us when you first enquire so a request can be made to CITB and Pearson VUE before your test is booked.

5. Is this an official CSCS or CITB website?

No. This page is operated by ConstructionsCert, an independent company that helps UK workers understand the process and book CSCS tests and cards. The CSCS scheme is owned by CSCS Ltd, and HS&E tests are delivered on behalf of CITB at Pearson VUE centres such as Wigan.
