About the CITB Operatives Test

The CITB Health, Safety & Environment Operatives Test is required for anyone applying for a CSCS Green Labourer Card. The test assesses your knowledge of health and safety practices on construction sites.

Test Format

  • 45-minute multiple-choice test
  • 50 questions covering health, safety and environment topics
  • Pass mark is 45 out of 50 (90%)
  • Results available immediately after completing the test

What to Bring

  • Valid photo ID (passport or driving licence)
  • Your booking confirmation
  • NI number (if you have one)

Next Steps After Your Test

Once you've passed your CITB Operatives Test, you'll be ready to apply for your CSCS Green Labourer Card. Here's what happens next:

  1. Receive your test pass notification (valid for 2 years)
  2. Complete your CSCS card application online or by phone
  3. Submit required documents and pay the application fee
  4. Receive your CSCS Green Labourer Card within 5-6 working days
  5. Start working legally and safely on UK construction sites
