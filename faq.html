What ID do I need at the UK test centre?

You must bring valid UK identification. Acceptable documents include a valid UK driving licence or signed UK/EU passport. If these aren't available, we can provide guidance on acceptable alternative documentation.

What should I expect during the CITB test in the UK?

The test consists of multiple-choice questions covering construction health and safety, and environmental awareness relevant to UK construction sites. The test duration is typically 45 minutes.

Are there revision materials available for the CITB test?

Yes, CSCS revision materials are available through CITB and various UK retailers. Ensure you obtain the latest version appropriate for your test level and occupation.

If I fail the CITB test, can I retake it?

Yes. After a 24-hour waiting period, you can rebook your test. Please note: CITB regulations stipulate that you cannot retest within 48 hours of your previous attempt.

What is the Test + Retest Option?

This option functions similarly to booking two tests. If you don't pass your initial attempt, we'll arrange new test dates for your retake. The 48-hour retest restriction still applies.

Why do I need to pass the CITB test for a CSCS card?

The CITB Health, Safety and Environment Test is a mandatory requirement for most CSCS cards in the UK. It validates that you possess essential health and safety knowledge required for working on UK construction sites, in compliance with Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015.

What other requirements are needed for a CSCS Card in the UK?

To apply for most CSCS cards in the United Kingdom, you typically need to:

  • Pass the appropriate CITB Health, Safety and Environment Test
  • Complete a Level 1 Award in Health and Safety in a Construction Environment (or equivalent SCQF Level 4 in Scotland)
  • Provide evidence of relevant construction qualifications or experience
  • Have a valid UK address for card delivery

What are the UK construction industry training requirements?

The UK construction industry operates under strict training requirements including:

  • Construction Skills Certification Scheme (CSCS) cards for site access
  • Construction Plant Competence Scheme (CPCS) for plant operators
  • NVQ/SVQ qualifications for skilled worker cards
  • Regular health and safety refresher training
  • Compliance with UK-specific regulations like CDM 2015

How long are CSCS cards valid for in the UK?

CSCS card validity periods vary by card type in the UK:

  • Green Labourer Cards: Typically 5 years
  • Blue Skilled Worker Cards: Typically 5 years
  • Gold Supervisor Cards: Typically 5 years
  • Red Trainee/Apprentice Cards: Valid for the duration of the apprenticeship
  • Black Manager Cards: Typically 5 years

All cards require a valid CITB test pass within the last 2 years for renewal.
