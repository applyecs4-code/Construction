About the CSCS White PQP Card

The CSCS White Professionally Qualified Person (PQP) Card is designed for construction professionals who hold membership with recognised professional bodies in the UK construction industry. This card validates both your professional competence and understanding of construction health and safety standards.

Specifically developed for architects, engineers, surveyors, project managers, and other professionally qualified individuals, the PQP Card provides formal recognition of your professional status while ensuring compliance with UK construction site safety requirements.

As principal contractors and clients increasingly require professional staff to hold appropriate CSCS cards, the PQP Card serves as essential certification for design, management, and consultancy roles in UK construction.

Eligibility Requirements

🏛️

Professional Membership

Current membership with a CSCS-approved professional body at the required level (e.g., RIBA, ICE, RICS, CIOB, IStructE).

✅

CITB HS&E Test

Valid pass certificate for the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers and Professionals (completed within last 2 years).

📄

Membership Evidence

CSCS membership certificate, current membership card, or verification letter from your professional body.

🇬🇧

UK Professional Practice

Must be practicing in or entering the UK construction sector with a verifiable UK address for professional correspondence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between PQP Card and other CSCS cards?

The PQP Card recognises professional body membership as evidence of competence, while other CSCS cards typically require vocational NVQs or academic qualifications. The PQP Card is specifically for professionally qualified staff with institutional memberships.

Do I need an NVQ or degree for the PQP Card?

No, the PQP Card route is based on professional body membership rather than academic or vocational qualifications. Your professional institution's membership requirements serve as the competence assessment.

How long is the PQP Card valid and how do I renew it?

The PQP Card is typically valid for 5 years. Renewal requires passing the latest CITB Managers & Professionals test and providing current evidence of your professional membership status.

What if my professional body is not UK-based?

International professional bodies may be considered if they are recognised as equivalent to UK professional institutions. Our professional team can advise on the recognition status of international memberships.

Can I upgrade from PQP Card to a different CSCS card?

Yes, you can progress to other CSCS cards as you gain additional qualifications and experience. Many professionals transition from PQP Cards to Black Manager Cards or other specialised cards as their careers progress.
