Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use group CSCS card booking?

Any company, contractor, or organization in the UK looking to book CSCS card tests or construction training for multiple employees can use our group booking service.

How long does it take to complete group bookings?

The process is quick. Once the application is submitted, confirmation is usually sent within 2-3 working days.

What is the price for group CSCS card booking?

The price for each CSCS card booked online through the group service is £65 per applicant.

Can we book different CSCS card types in a single group booking?

Yes, you can choose multiple CSCS card types for your team and complete the group booking together.

Do you provide CITB Health & Safety training for teams?
